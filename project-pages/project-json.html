in this small experiment the goal was to use json data to generate visual artifacts. wether it was going the be usefull or just visually appealling was up to us.

since working with json datasets quickly becomes very complicated, i chose a small dataset with the name "health and social structure atlas in berlin 2022". it compares the quality of life in different districts in berlin. the data is based on indicators such as the unemployment rate, the poverty risk rate and life expectancy.

these in turn produce a value that i used to determine the radius and the fluctuations of the circles of the districts. The smoother the circle, the better the district is doing. This allows you to compare them very quickly and easily at a glance.

however, only an overall picture is shown here. to expand the code, i would like to add deeper levels. by clicking on a district you could compare the different indices and get a detailed insight into the reasons for the overall rating.
