the goal of the tictactoe task was to code an interpretation of the minigame to further practice arrays, conditions and functions.

i wanted to experiment with images and try out how they can be included in p5sketches.

it was very tricky to capture the random value that alternates the images and use it to draw the "selected" image on the canvas. the problem was that the background grid had to be redrawn over and over again so that the cursor images were not constantly drawn on the canvas.

the next step would be to build it for two players. my idea was to select between player 1 or 2 with a button or by pressing a specific key while clicking. it would also be nice that as soon as the first image is drawn, it is saved and drawn with each subsequent click.
